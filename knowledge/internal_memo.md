# Internal Memo: 2026 Summer Project Launch and Collaboration Guidelines

**Date:** June 25, 2026
**Department:** Operations Management
**To:** All Staff

In support of our Q3 business objectives, the company is launching the "Summer Performance Optimization Project." Please adhere to the following guidelines to ensure smooth and efficient team collaboration:

* **Process Simplification:** Minimize non-essential cross-departmental meetings; prioritize real-time messaging for synchronization.
* **Goal Alignment:** All project deliverables must be submitted for internal validation by August 31.

* **Transparent Communication:** All project progress must be updated on the internal collaboration platform by the end of each workday.
* **Response Time:** Cross-departmental email responses are expected within 4 business hours to ensure information flow.
* **Proactive Reporting:** If obstacles arise, report them to the project team by 12:00 PM daily.

### 3. Key Reminders

* Department managers must submit the proposed manpower allocation chart to the General Affairs team by Friday, June 26.
* An "Efficiency Incentive Program" will be implemented; top-performing teams will receive additional subsidies.
